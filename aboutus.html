At Black Canyon Design, we build custom furnishing for every area of the home. Our designs include fully custom and semi-custom Home Theater Seating, refined Western Lodge chairs, sofas and tables, and a more contemporary group featuring classic lines with an updated twist.

All pieces are constructed of hardwoods then, depending on the particular design, other materials will be used such as decorative nails, leather braiding, and forged metals. We also make extensive use of exotic woods to create a truly distinctive piece of furniture.
